KBC is located at LOT 3418 A, Tingkat Bawah, Gertak Serong, Tendong, 17030 Pasir Mas, Kelantan. This company used online, offline and shop as a medium of their business. For online business, it has about 80 drop ship from different state such as Kuala Lumpur, Perak, Negeri Sembilan and Johor. There are few types of online shop which is facebook, lazada, shopee and instagram. For offline and shop there are 13 worker from different department. For example, 8 worker from operation department and others from department sales, marketing, admin, human resource and financial.
